\textbf{Question}:\\
Draw a triangle $ABC$ in which $BC = 7cm$, and $\angle B = 45\degree$, $\angle{C}=60^\circ$.\\
\solution \\

Find $\angle{A}$
\begin{align}
     \angle{A}+\angle{B}+\angle{C}=180^\circ 
\end{align}    
\begin{align}    
     \angle{A}=75^\circ
\end{align}
Using the Sine Rule,
\begin{align}
    \frac{a}{\sin{A}}=\frac{b}{\sin{B}}=\frac{c}{\sin{C}}
    \label{0.3}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
    b=\frac{a \sin B}{\sin A} 
\end{align}
\begin{align}
    b=\frac{14\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}
    \label{0.5}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
    c=\frac{a \sin C}{\sin A}
\end{align}
\begin{align}
      c= \frac{14\sqrt3}{\sqrt6+\sqrt2}
      \label{0.7}
\end{align}
Therefore measure of the sides are, \ref{0.5} \ref{0.7} \\
